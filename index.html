Sitakunda Travel:

Hill, Waterfall, Beach - All In One Place!

Sitakunda (সীতাকুন্ড) is an upazila in Chittagong which is currently at the peak of popularity among tourists. Fountains, mountains, Waterfalls, Beach, clouds, you can enjoy everything from here? Actually, this is the reason why innumerable tourists are going to Sitakundo every day. Some are camping while others are trekking and coming back day after day. Sitakunda has gained more popularity as it is possible to return from Dhaka, Chittagong, Feni, Comilla or any other district in Bangladesh by day trip.harjhari Jharna Trail, komoldoho Jharna Trail and Sitakunda Ecopark. A traveler must stay for a few days to see all the sights of Sitakunda and its surroundings. If one starts the trail from Feni, it will take him 3 days to see the Boro komoldoho waterfalls, Choto komoldoho waterfalls, napittachara, khayachara, sahasradhara and suptadhara lasyamayi springs. Sitakunda has Banshbaria and Guliyakhali beaches. In addition to all this, if you want to trek separately in Hazarikhil or Baraiadhala forest, it will take more time, right? The hill of holy Chandranath is also not to be missed. From the capital Dhaka, Comilla, Narsingdi, Feni, Chittagong or its environs, you can easily reach to Sitakunda in one day. However, it is not possible to visit all the places of Sitakunda in one day. Although Khayachara Jharna is located in Mirsarai Upazila, Khayachara Jharna has also been included in our tour plan due to its proximity to Sitakunda and for a day trip experience of nature with mountains, waterfalls and sea. For a day trip you can choose to travel to other places in Sitakunda as you wish.
